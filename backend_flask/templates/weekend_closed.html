A Note from the Kitchen

A Brief Intermission.

Our kitchen observes Fridays and Saturdays as days of quiet repose — the better to return on Sunday with the care your appetite deserves.

We understand the craving. It is, frankly, flattering.

Until Sunday. Bring that appetite with you.
