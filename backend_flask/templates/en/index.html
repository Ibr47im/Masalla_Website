Who We Are

A New Standard in Food Innovation

Masalla is a brand-new food CPU based in Riyadh, revolutionizing the B2B and B2G sectors with diverse, world-class food services. Operating from a state-of-the-art central facility, we combine culinary expertise with efficient logistics.
